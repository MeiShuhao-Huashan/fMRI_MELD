Multiplicity correction: **p_adj (multiplicity)** reports multiplicity-adjusted p-values using a hierarchical scheme: Holm–Bonferroni adjustment for the **primary family** of 3 endpoints (primary + co-primary + pinpointing), and Benjamini–Hochberg false discovery rate adjustment for the **secondary family** of the remaining metrics.

P-value formatting follows Epilepsia guidance: p-values > 0.01 are shown with two decimal places; 0.001 ≤ p ≤ 0.01 with three decimal places; p < 0.001 is reported as <0.001.
